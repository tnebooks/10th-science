   - a) movement of molecules from lower to higher concentration
   - b) expenditure of energy
   - c) it is an uphill task
   - d) all of the above

   - a) cortex
   - b) epidermis
   - c) phloem

3. During transpiration there is loss of
   - a) carbon dioxide
   - b) oxygen
   - c) water

4. Root hairs are
   - a) cortical cell
   - b) projection of epidermal cell
   - c) unicellular
   - d) both b and c
