---
choices:
  - Hygroscopic
  - Dehydration
  - Dissociation
answers:
  - Deliquescence
explanation: >-
  Sodium hydroxide is a deliquescent substance. When exposed to the atmosphere, 
  it absorbs enough moisture from the air to completely dissolve and form a 
  saturated liquid solution. While hygroscopic substances also absorb 
  moisture, they do not dissolve into a liquid state like deliquescent ones do.
