answers:
  - thermal expansion of liquids
explanation: >-
  Volume percentage is defined as the volume of solute per volume of solution. 
  When temperature increases, liquids undergo thermal expansion, causing the 
  total volume of the solution to increase. Since the volume of the solution 
  (the denominator) increases while the amount of solute remains the same, 
  the overall volume percentage decreases.
