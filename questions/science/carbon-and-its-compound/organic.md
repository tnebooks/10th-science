The IUPAC name of an organic compound is 3-Methyl butan-1-ol. What type compound it is?

```markdown
  The suffix '-ol' in the IUPAC name indicates the presence of a hydroxyl 
  group ($-OH$), which is the characteristic functional group of alcohols. 
  In this case, the '1-ol' tells us the alcohol group is on the first carbon 
  of the butane chain.
```
