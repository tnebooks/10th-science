  - "afforestion"
explanation: "Soil erosion is the displacement of the upper layer of soil. Afforestation (planting trees in an area where there was no previous tree cover) and reforestation help prevent erosion because tree roots bind the soil particles together, while the canopy reduces the direct impact of rain on the ground."
