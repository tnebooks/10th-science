An inexhaustible resources is:


```markdown
  Inexhaustible resources are those that are available in unlimited quantities
  in nature and are not likely to be exhausted by human activities. Wind power
  is driven by solar heating and the Earth's rotation, making it truly
  inexhaustible. In contrast, soil fertility and wildlife are 'renewable' but
  exhaustible; they can be depleted or destroyed if overused or mismanaged.
```
