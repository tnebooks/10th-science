Global warming will cause:


```markdown
  Global warming leads to an increase in Earth's average temperature, which
  triggers a chain reaction: heat causes glaciers and polar ice caps to melt,
  adding more water to the oceans. Additionally, water expands as it warms
  (thermal expansion), further raising sea levels. This combined rise in ocean
  levels poses a direct threat to low-lying islands and coastal regions,
  eventually leading to them sinking or becoming uninhabitable.
```
