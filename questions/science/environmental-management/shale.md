The soft finely stratified sedimentary rock refers to

```markdown
  Shale is a fine-grained, clastic sedimentary rock formed from the 
  accumulation of silt and clay-sized mineral particles. It is 
  characterized by its ability to split into thin layers, a property 
  known as fissility, making it "finely stratified."
```
