What are the steps will you adopt for better waste management?


```markdown
  Effective waste management follows the '3Rs' hierarchy: Reduce, Reuse, and
  Recycle. Reducing waste at the source is the most effective strategy, followed
  by reusing items to extend their lifespan, and finally recycling materials to
  create new products. Combining all three steps is the best way to minimize
  environmental impact.
```
