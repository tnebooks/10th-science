The energy obtained from the movement of water due to ocean tides is -----------

```markdown
  Tidal energy is a form of hydropower that converts the energy obtained 
  from tides into useful forms of power, mainly electricity. It is 
  generated by the surge of ocean waters during the rise and fall of 
  tides, which are caused by the gravitational forces of the Moon and 
  the Sun.
```
