 Which of the following is / are a fossil fuel?
  i. Tar ii. Coal iii. Petroleum

```markdown
Fossil fuels are formed from the anaerobic decomposition of buried dead organisms over millions of years. Coal is formed from ancient plant matter, while petroleum (crude oil) and natural gas come from marine organisms. Tar (bitumen) is a thick, sticky form of petroleum, often found in oil sands, and is therefore also considered a fossil fuel.
```
