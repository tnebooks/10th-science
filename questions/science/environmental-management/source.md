explanation: >-
  Trees (biomass) are considered a renewable source of energy because they can
  be replanted and grown again within a relatively short human timeframe. In
  contrast, petroleum and coal are fossil fuels that take millions of years to
  form, and nuclear fuel (uranium) is a finite mineral resource.
