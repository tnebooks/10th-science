Green house effect refers to:


```markdown
  The greenhouse effect is a natural process where certain gases in Earth's
  atmosphere (like CO2 and methane) trap heat radiating from the planet's
  surface. This heat, which would otherwise escape into space, is reflected back
  toward Earth, leading to an increase in the average surface temperature. While
  the term 'greenhouse' is used in plant cultivation, in an environmental
  context, it specifically refers to this warming phenomenon.
```
