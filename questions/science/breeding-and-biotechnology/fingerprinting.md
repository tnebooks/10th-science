DNA fingerprinting is based on the principle of identifying _______ sequences of DNA


```markdown
  DNA fingerprinting is based on identifying repetitive DNA sequences,
  specifically Variable Number Tandem Repeats (VNTRs) or Short Tandem Repeats
  (STRs). These repetitive sequences vary greatly between individuals (except
  identical twins), making them ideal for identification purposes.
```
