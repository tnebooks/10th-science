Which method of crop improvement can be practised by a farmer if he is inexperienced?


```markdown
  Mass selection is the simplest and oldest method of crop improvement that can
  be practiced by inexperienced farmers. In this method, the farmer selects a
  large number of visually superior plants based on observable traits
  (phenotype) and bulks their seeds for planting the next generation. It
  requires no technical knowledge of genetics or breeding.
```
