answer:
  - "mass selection"
explanation: "Mass selection is the simplest and oldest method of crop improvement that can be practiced by inexperienced farmers. In this method, the farmer selects a large number of visually superior plants based on observable traits (phenotype) and bulks their seeds for planting the next generation. It requires no technical knowledge of genetics or breeding."
