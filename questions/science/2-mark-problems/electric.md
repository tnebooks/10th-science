If 12 coulombs of charge flows through an electric bulb in 5 seconds, find the electric current.

```markdown
Electric current is given by:
$I = \frac{Q}{t}$

Given:
$Q = 12 \text{ C}$
$t = 5 \text{ s}$

Substituting:
$I = \frac{12}{5}$

$I = 2.4 \text{ A}$

Therefore:
Electric current, $I = 2.4 \text{ A}$
```
