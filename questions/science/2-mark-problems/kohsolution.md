Calculate the pH of a $1.0 \times 10^{-5}$ M $KOH$ solution.

```markdown
pOH formula:
$pOH = -\log_{10}[OH^-]$

Given:
$[OH^-] = 1.0 \times 10^{-5}$

Substituting:
$pOH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-5})$

$pOH = 5$

Relation between pH and pOH:
$pH + pOH = 14$

$pH = 14 - pOH$

$pH = 14 - 5$

$pH = 9$

Therefore:
pH value = 9
```
