If a current of 2 A flows through a conductor with a potential difference of 30 V, find its resistance.

```markdown
Ohm's law:
$R = \frac{V}{I}$

Given:
$V = 30 \text{ V}$
$I = 2 \text{ A}$

Substituting:
$R = \frac{30}{2}$

$R = 15 \ \Omega$

Therefore:
Resistance, $R = 15 \ \Omega$
```
