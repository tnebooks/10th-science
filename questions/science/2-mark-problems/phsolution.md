Calculate the pH of a $1.0 \times 10^{-4}$ M $HNO_3$ solution.

```markdown
pH formula:
$pH = -\log_{10}[H^+]$

Given:
$[H^+] = 1.0 \times 10^{-4}$

Substituting:
$pH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-4})$

$\log_{10}(10^{-4}) = -4$

$pH = -(-4)$

$pH = 4$

Therefore:
pH value = 4
```
