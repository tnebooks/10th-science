Calculate the total energy released when a radioactive substance of mass 2 kg undergoes nuclear reaction.

```markdown
Einstein’s mass-energy relation:
$E = mc^2$

Given:
$m = 2 \text{ kg}$
$c = 3 \times 10^8 \text{ m s}^{-1}$

Substituting:
$E = 2 \times (3 \times 10^8)^2$

$E = 2 \times 9 \times 10^{16}$

$E = 18 \times 10^{16} \text{ J}$

$E = 1.8 \times 10^{17} \text{ J}$

Therefore:
Total energy, $E = 1.8 \times 10^{17} \text{ J}$
```
