Calculate the pH of a 0.01 M $HNO_3$ solution.

```markdown
$HNO_3$ is a strong acid, so:
$[H^+] = 1.0 \times 10^{-2}$

pH formula:
$pH = -\log_{10}[H^+]$

Substituting:
$pH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-2})$

$\log_{10}(10^{-2}) = -2$

$pH = -(-2)$

$pH = 2$

Therefore:
pH value = 2
```
