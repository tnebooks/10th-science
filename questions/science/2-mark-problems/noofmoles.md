Find the number of moles:

(a) 27 g of Aluminium  
(b) $1.51 \times 10^{23}$ molecules of $NH_4Cl$

```markdown
Formula for number of moles:
$\text{Moles} = \frac{\text{Mass}}{\text{Atomic mass}}$

(a) Aluminium:
Mass = 27 g  
Atomic mass = 27  

Moles = $\frac{27}{27} = 1 \text{ mole}$

(b) Molecules:
$\text{Moles} = \frac{\text{Number of molecules}}{\text{Avogadro number}}$

$\text{Moles} = \frac{1.51 \times 10^{23}}{6.023 \times 10^{23}}$

$\text{Moles} = 0.25 \text{ mole}$

Therefore:
(a) 1 mole  
(b) 0.25 mole
```
