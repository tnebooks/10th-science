```markdown
  Cytokinins are plant hormones that promote cell division (cytokinesis). 
  They are found in high concentrations in areas where rapid cell division 
  occurs, such as the liquid endosperm of coconut (coconut water), which 
  supports the growth of the developing embryo.
```
