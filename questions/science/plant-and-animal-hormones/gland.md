Pancreas acts as ------- gland

```markdown
  The pancreas is a dual-purpose organ known as a heterocrine gland. It 
  acts as an exocrine gland by secreting digestive enzymes through ducts 
  into the duodenum, and as an endocrine gland by secreting hormones like 
  insulin and glucagon directly into the bloodstream.
```
