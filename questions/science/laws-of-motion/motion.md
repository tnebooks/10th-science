Impulse is equals to ___ .


```markdown
  Impulse (J) is defined as the product of force and time (J = F × Δt).
  According to Newton's second law, F = Δp/Δt, therefore F × Δt = Δp. This means
  impulse equals the change in momentum of an object. Rate of change of momentum
  is equal to force, not impulse.
```
