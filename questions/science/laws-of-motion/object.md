choices:
  - "weight of the object"
  - "acceleration due to gravity of the planet"
  - "mass of the object"
  - "density of the object"
answer:
  - "mass of the object"
explanation: "Inertia is the inherent property of a body that resists any change in its state of rest or motion. It depends solely on the mass of the object - greater mass means greater inertia. Weight depends on gravity and varies by location, acceleration due to gravity is a planetary property, and density is mass per unit volume which does not determine inertia."
