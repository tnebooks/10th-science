The force required to produce an acceleration of 1 cm s−2 on a body of mass 1 g is

```markdown
  Force is calculated using the formula $F = m \times a$. In the CGS system, 
  the unit of force is the dyne, where $1 \text{ dyne} = 1 \text{ g} \cdot \text{cm/s}^2$. 
  Since the mass is $1 \text{ g}$ and the acceleration is $1 \text{ cm/s}^2$, the 
  force is exactly $1 \text{ dyne}$.
```
