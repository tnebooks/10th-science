answer:
  - "increase by 300%"
explanation: "Weight is given by W = GMm/R². If Earth's radius becomes R' = R/2 (50% of original) while mass remains constant, the new weight W' = GMm/(R/2)² = GMm/(R²/4) = 4 × GMm/R² = 4W. The weight becomes 4 times the original value. An increase from W to 4W represents an increase of (4W - W)/W × 100% = 300%. Therefore, the weight increases by 300% (or becomes 400% of the original)."
