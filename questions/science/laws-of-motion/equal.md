answer:
  - "9.8 × 10⁵ dyne"
explanation: "One kilogram force (kgf) is the force exerted by gravity on a mass of 1 kg at Earth's surface. Using F = mg, where m = 1 kg and g = 9.8 m/s², we get F = 9.8 N. To convert Newtons to dynes (CGS unit), we use 1 N = 10⁵ dyne. Therefore, 9.8 N = 9.8 × 10⁵ dyne. The other options are incorrect: 9.8 dyne is far too small, 9.8 × 10⁴ N is in wrong unit and magnitude, and 980 dyne equals only 0.0098 N."
