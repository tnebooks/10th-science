The effective resistance of three resistors of resistances 5Ω, 3Ω and 2Ω are connected in series is ______

```markdown
  In a series circuit, the effective resistance ($R_s$) is the sum of the 
  individual resistances: $R_s = R_1 + R_2 + R_3$. 
  Substituting the given values: $5\Omega + 3\Omega + 2\Omega = 10\Omega$.
```
