```markdown
  Kilowatt-hour (kWh) is a unit of energy. Power is the rate of energy 
  consumption ($P = E/t$), so energy is power multiplied by time ($E = P \times t$). 
  When a power of 1 kilowatt is used for 1 hour, the energy consumed is 1 kWh.
```
