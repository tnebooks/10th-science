A charge of 12 coulomb flows through a bulb in 5 second. What is the current through
the bulb?

```markdown
  Electric current ($I$) is defined as the rate of flow of charge ($Q$),($t$) - "time . Using the formula $I = Q / t$, we substitute the given "
  values: $12 \text{ C} / 5 \text{ s} = 2.4 \text{ A}$.
```
