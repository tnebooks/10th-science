SI unit of specific resistance is ______

```markdown
  Specific resistance, or resistivity ($\rho$), is defined by the formula 
  $\rho = R \cdot A / L$. Since the unit of resistance ($R$) is ohm ($\Omega$), 
  area ($A$) is $m^2$, and length ($L$) is $m$, the SI unit becomes 
  $\Omega \cdot m^2 / m = \Omega \cdot m$ (ohm metre).
```
