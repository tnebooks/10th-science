Variation in dimensions of any object due to rise in temperature is called as __

```markdown
  Thermal expansion is the tendency of matter to change its shape, area, 
  volume, and density in response to a change in temperature. When a 
  substance is heated, its molecules vibrate more vigorously, leading to 
  an increase in the average separation between them.
```
