If the atoms or molecules of a gas do not interact with each other, then the gas is said to be

```markdown
  An ideal gas is a theoretical gas composed of many randomly moving point 
  particles that are not subject to interparticle interactions. In contrast, 
  real gas molecules do have small attractive forces and occupy a finite 
  amount of space.
```
