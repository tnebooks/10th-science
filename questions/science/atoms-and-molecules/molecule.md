choices:
  - "Glucose"
  - "Helium"
  - "Carbon dioxide"
  - "Hydrogen"
answer:
  - "Carbon dioxide"
explanation: "A triatomic molecule is composed of exactly three atoms. Carbon dioxide ($CO_2$) consists of one carbon atom and two oxygen atoms ($1 + 2 = 3$). In contrast, Helium is monoatomic (1 atom), Hydrogen gas ($H_2$) is diatomic (2 atoms), and Glucose ($C_6H_{12}O_6$) is polyatomic (24 atoms)."
