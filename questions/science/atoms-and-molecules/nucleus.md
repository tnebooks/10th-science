answers:
  - 20 protons and 20 neutrons
explanation: >-
  In the notation $_{Z}X^{A}$, Z is the atomic number (number of protons) and A
  is the mass number (protons + neutrons). For Calcium ($_{20}Ca^{40}$), the
  atomic number is 20, meaning there are 20 protons. The number of neutrons is
  calculated as Mass Number - Atomic Number ($40 - 20 = 20$). Electrons are not
  located in the nucleus.
