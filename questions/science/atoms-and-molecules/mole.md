1 mole of any substance contains ____ molecules.


```markdown
  One mole of any substance is defined as the amount of that substance that
  contains as many elementary entities (atoms, molecules, ions, etc.) as there
  are atoms in exactly 12 grams of carbon-12. This fixed number is known as
  Avogadro's number, which is approximately $6.023 \times 10^{23}$.
```
