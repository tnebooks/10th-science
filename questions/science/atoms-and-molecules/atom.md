Mass of 1 mole of Nitrogen atom is:


```markdown
  The atomic mass of Nitrogen (N) is 14 atomic mass units (amu). This represents
  the mass of a single nitrogen atom. 1 amu is defined as 1/12th the mass of a
  carbon-12 atom. While 14 g represents the molar mass (mass of 1 mole of
  nitrogen atoms), and 28 g represents the molar mass of nitrogen gas (N₂), the
  question specifically asks for the mass at the atomic level, which is 14 amu.
```
