Which of the following has the smallest mass?


```markdown
  1 helium atom has the least mass among the given options. 6.023 × 10²³ helium
  atoms equal 1 mole (4 g). 2 g helium is half a mole (3.01 × 10²³ atoms). 1
  mole helium atom equals 4 g. A single helium atom has a mass of approximately
  4 amu (6.64 × 10⁻²⁴ g), which is the smallest mass.
```
