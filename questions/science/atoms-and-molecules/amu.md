  - Mass of a hydrogen atom
  - Mass of O-16 atom
answers:
  - 1/12th of the mass of a C-12 atom
explanation: >-
  The Atomic Mass Unit (amu) is defined based on the Carbon-12 isotope. Since a
  Carbon-12 atom contains exactly 12 nucleons (6 protons and 6 neutrons), 1 amu
  is defined as exactly 1/12th of the mass of a single carbon-12 atom. This
  provides a standard reference for measuring the relative masses of all other
  elements.
