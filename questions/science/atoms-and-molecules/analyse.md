11. Analyse the following and choose the correct statement(s)
i) An electron has considerable mass
ii) A hetero atomic molecule is formed from different kinds of atoms.
iii) Mass number and atomic mass of an element are same

```markdown
  Statement (i) is incorrect because electrons have negligible mass 
  compared to protons and neutrons. Statement (ii) is correct as heteroatomic 
  molecules (like $H_2O$) consist of different elements. Statement (iii) is 
  incorrect because mass number is an integer (protons + neutrons), while 
  atomic mass is a weighted average that usually includes decimals.
```
