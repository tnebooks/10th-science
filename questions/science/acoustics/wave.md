choices:
  - speed
  - frequency
  - wavelength
answers:
  - none of these
explanation: >-
  When a sound wave is reflected into the same medium, its fundamental
  characteristics remain unchanged. The speed depends on the medium, which has
  not changed. The frequency is determined by the source, which remains
  constant. Since both speed and frequency are constant, the wavelength (λ =
  v/f) also remains the same. Only the direction of propagation changes.
