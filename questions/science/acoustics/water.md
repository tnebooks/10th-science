Arrange the following media in descending order on the basis of speed of sound

```markdown
  The speed of sound depends on the density and elasticity of the medium. 
  Sound travels fastest in solids (glass), slower in liquids (water), and 
  slowest in gases (air). Therefore, the descending order is 
  $\text{Solids} > \text{Liquids} > \text{Gases}$.
```
