There are ________ pairs of cranial nerves and ________ pairs of spinal nerves.


```markdown
  In the human peripheral nervous system, there are 12 pairs of cranial nerves
  that emerge directly from the brain and 31 pairs of spinal nerves that emerge
  from the spinal cord. These nerves carry sensory and motor information between
  the central nervous system and the rest of the body.
```
