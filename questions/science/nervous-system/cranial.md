  - duramater
explanation: >-
  The brain and spinal cord are protected by three layers of membranes called
  meninges. From the outermost layer to the innermost, they are: the Dura mater
  (tough and fibrous), the Arachnoid mater (web-like), and the Pia mater (thin
  and delicate, in direct contact with the brain). The myelin sheath is an
  insulating layer around axons, not a meningeal layer.
