  - 'away from, towards'
answers:
  - 'towards, away from'
explanation: >-
  In a neuron, the flow of an electrical impulse is unidirectional. Dendrites
  are the branching structures that receive signals from other neurons and carry
  them towards the cell body (cyton). The axon is the long fiber that carries
  the processed impulse away from the cell body toward the next neuron or an
  effector organ.
