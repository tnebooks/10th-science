Node of Ranvier is found in:


```markdown
  Nodes of Ranvier are the periodic gaps found in the myelin sheath that covers
  the axons of many neurons. These gaps facilitate the rapid conduction of nerve
  impulses through a process called saltatory conduction, where the electrical
  signal 'jumps' from one node to the next.
```
