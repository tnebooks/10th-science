Bipolar neurons are found in:


```markdown
  Bipolar neurons are specialized sensory neurons that have two processes
  extending from the cell body: one axon and one dendrite. They are relatively
  rare and are primarily found in specialized sensory organs, most notably the
  retina of the eye, as well as the olfactory epithelium (nose) and the inner
  ear.
```
