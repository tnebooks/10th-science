answers:
  - hypothalamus
explanation: >-
  The hypothalamus is the master regulator of homeostasis in the body. It
  contains specialized centers that monitor and control body temperature
  (thermoregulation), thirst and water balance (osmoregulation), and hunger
  (appetite). Damage to this area results in the loss of control over these
  vital involuntary internal conditions.
