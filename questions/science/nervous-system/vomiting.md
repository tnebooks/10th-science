Vomiting centre is located in:


```markdown
  The vomiting center is a specific region in the medulla oblongata of the
  brainstem. It receives sensory inputs from the digestive tract, the vestibular
  system (balance), and the chemoreceptor trigger zone (CTZ) to coordinate the
  complex muscular actions required for vomiting. While the stomach is where the
  action occurs, the control center is in the brain.
```
