```markdown
  Sarcolemma is the specific name given to the cell membrane of a muscle fiber.
  Nerve cells (neurons) have a cell membrane often referred to as the neurolemma
  (or neurilemma when referring to the sheath of Schwann cells), but the term
  'sarcolemma' is exclusive to muscular tissue.
```
