If the electronegativity difference between two bonded atoms in a molecule is greater than 1.7 then the nature of bonding is ___________

```markdown
  The electronegativity difference between two atoms determines the bond 
  type. According to the Pauling scale, if the difference is greater than 1.7, 
  the bond is considered ionic. If it is less than 1.7, the bond is covalent, 
  and if it is exactly 1.7, the bond is 50% ionic and 50% covalent.
```
