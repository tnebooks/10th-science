_____ is a relative periodic property

```markdown
  Electronegativity is considered a relative property because it does not 
  have a specific unit and is measured on a scale (like the Pauling scale) 
  based on an atom's ability to attract a shared pair of electrons in a 
  chemical bond relative to other atoms.
```
