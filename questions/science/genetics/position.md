In metacentric chromosome, the position of centromere is

```markdown
  In a metacentric chromosome, the centromere is located exactly in the 
  middle (the centre). This results in two arms of equal length, giving 
  the chromosome a characteristic 'V' shape during the anaphase stage 
  of cell division.
```
