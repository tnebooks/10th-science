9:3:3:1 ratio is due to:


```markdown
  The 9:3:3:1 phenotypic ratio is the classic result of a Mendelian dihybrid
  cross. It occurs because of the Law of Independent Assortment, which states
  that different pairs of alleles (for different traits like seed shape and
  color) separate independently of one another during the formation of gametes.
```
