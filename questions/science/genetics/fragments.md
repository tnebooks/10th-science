choices:
  - Helicase
  - DNA polymerase
  - RNA primer
answers:
  - DNA ligase
explanation: >-
  Okazaki fragments are short sequences of DNA nucleotides which are synthesized
  discontinuously on the lagging strand during DNA replication. DNA ligase is
  the enzyme responsible for 'gluing' these fragments together by creating a
  phosphodiester bond between the 3' end of one fragment and the 5' end of the
  next, resulting in a continuous DNA strand.
