Identify Dihybrid ratio


```markdown
  The 9:3:3:1 ratio is the classic phenotypic ratio resulting from a  Mendelian
  dihybrid cross (e.g., $RrYy \times RrYy$). It represents the  relative
  proportions of the four possible phenotype combinations:  dominant for both
  traits (9), dominant for the first but recessive  for the second (3),
  recessive for the first but dominant for the  second (3), and recessive for
  both traits (1).
```
