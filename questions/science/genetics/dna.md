The ________ units form the backbone of the DNA.


```markdown
  The 'backbone' of the DNA double helix is a repeating structure composed of
  alternating sugar (deoxyribose) and phosphate groups. While the nitrogenous
  bases face inward to form the 'rungs' of the ladder, the sugar-phosphate units
  provide the structural framework on the outside.
```
