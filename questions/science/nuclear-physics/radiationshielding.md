```markdown
  Lead (Pb) is commonly used as a shielding material against radiation due to
  its high density (11.34 g/cm³) and high atomic number (82). It effectively
  absorbs gamma rays and X-rays, making it ideal for radiation protection in
  medical facilities, nuclear reactors, and laboratories. While lead oxide is
  also used in some applications, metallic lead is the primary shielding
  material.
```
