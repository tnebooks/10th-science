Artificial radioactivity was discovered by ____________


```markdown
  Artificial radioactivity (induced radioactivity) was discovered in 1934 by
  Irène Curie and her husband Frédéric Joliot when they bombarded aluminum with
  alpha particles to produce radioactive phosphorus-30. They were awarded the
  Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1935 for this discovery.
```
