In which of the following reaction, the mass number decreases by four of the
daughter nucleus?

```markdown
  In alpha ($\alpha$) decay, the nucleus emits an alpha particle, which 
  consists of two protons and two neutrons (equivalent to a Helium nucleus). 
  Because four nucleons are removed, the mass number ($A$) of the daughter 
  nucleus decreases by exactly four.
```
