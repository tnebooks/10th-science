Gamma radiations are dangerous because


```markdown
  Genetic disorders (hereditary defects) are caused by mutations in DNA due to
  radiation exposure. When ionizing radiation damages the genetic material in
  reproductive cells (sperm or egg cells), these mutations can be passed on to
  offspring, resulting in birth defects or genetic diseases in future
  generations. This is different from somatic effects which affect only the
  exposed individual.
```
