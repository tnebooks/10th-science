In Beta decay

```markdown
  In $\beta^-$ (beta minus) decay, a neutron in the nucleus is transformed 
  into a proton and an electron. The electron (beta particle) is emitted. 
  As a result, the number of protons increases by one, which also means 
  the atomic number increases by one.
```
