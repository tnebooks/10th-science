Unit of radioactivity is _____________


```markdown
  Rontgen (R) is the SI unit of exposure for X-rays and gamma rays, measuring
  ionization in air. Curie (Ci) is the traditional unit of radioactivity (1 Ci =
  3.7 × 10¹⁰ disintegrations per second), though becquerel (Bq) is now the SI
  unit (1 Bq = 1 disintegration per second). Becquerel (Bq) is the SI unit of
  activity. All three are recognized units in radiation measurement, with
  Becquerel being the fundamental SI unit for activity.
```
