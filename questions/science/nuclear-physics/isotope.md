____________ isotope is used for the treatment of cancer.


```markdown
  Radioactive cobalt-60 (Co-60) is widely used in cancer treatment
  (radiotherapy) due to its high-energy gamma ray emission (1.17 MeV and 1.33
  MeV). It is used in teletherapy machines (cobalt therapy) to treat deep
  tumors. Radio iodine (I-131) is used for thyroid disorders, radio carbon
  (C-14) is used in carbon dating and tracer studies, and radio nickel is not
  commonly used in medical applications.
```
