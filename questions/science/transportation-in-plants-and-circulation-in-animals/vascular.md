  - "xylem"
explanation: "Xylem is a specialized vascular tissue in plants responsible for the conduction of water and dissolved minerals from the roots up to the leaves and other aerial parts. It consists of tracheids and vessels that form a continuous system of tubes. In contrast, phloem is responsible for transporting food (glucose) from the leaves to other parts of the plant."
