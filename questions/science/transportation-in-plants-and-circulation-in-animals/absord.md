Structure in roots that help to absorb water is _________

```markdown
  Root hairs are fine, tubular outgrowths of the epidermal cells of the root. 
  They significantly increase the surface area for absorption and are 
  responsible for taking up water and minerals from the soil through 
  the process of osmosis.
```
