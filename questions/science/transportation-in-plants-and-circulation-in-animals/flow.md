 Which is the correct sequence of blood flow?

```markdown
In the human circulatory system, blood flows in a specific one-way direction. It first enters the heart at the atrium, moves down into the ventricle, is pumped out through the arteries to the body or lungs, and finally returns to the heart through the veins. Therefore, the sequence is Atrium → Ventricle → Arteries → Veins.
```
