The heart of amphibians possess __________ chambers.


```markdown
  Amphibians have a three-chambered heart consisting of two atria and one 
  ventricle. This structure allows for a "double circulation" system where 
  oxygenated and deoxygenated blood are kept somewhat separate in the atria  but
  mix slightly in the single ventricle.
```
