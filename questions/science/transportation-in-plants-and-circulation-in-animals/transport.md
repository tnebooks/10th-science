  Active transport involves:

```markdown
Active transport is the process of moving molecules across a cell membrane against their concentration gradient (from low to high concentration). Because this movement is 'uphill'—meaning it goes against the natural flow of diffusion—it requires the expenditure of energy, typically in the form of ATP.
```
