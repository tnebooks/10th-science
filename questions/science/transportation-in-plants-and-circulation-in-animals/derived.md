The concept of blood group is derived by ____.

```markdown
  The concept of blood groups was discovered by Karl Landsteiner in 1900. 
  He identified the ABO blood group system by observing the agglutination 
  of red blood cells when mixed with different sera, which earned him 
  the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine in 1930.
```
