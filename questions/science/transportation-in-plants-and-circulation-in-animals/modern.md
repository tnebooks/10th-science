Who is regarded as the “Father of Modern Physiology”

```markdown
  William Harvey is regarded as the Father of Modern Physiology because he 
  was the first to accurately describe the systemic circulation and 
  properties of blood being pumped to the brain and body by the heart. 
  Karl Landsteiner, for comparison, is known for discovering the ABO 
  blood groups.
```
