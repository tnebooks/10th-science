 Which of the following process requires energy?

```markdown
Active transport is the only process among the choices that requires cellular energy (ATP). It moves substances against their concentration gradient. Diffusion and osmosis are types of passive transport, meaning they occur naturally without the expenditure of energy, moving substances from areas of high concentration to areas of low concentration.
```
