  Which one of the following shows correct composition of blood?

```markdown
Whole blood is composed of a liquid component called plasma (about 55%) and formed elements (about 45%), which include Red Blood Cells (Erythrocytes), White Blood Cells (Leukocytes), and Platelets (Thrombocytes). Option B is incorrect because Serum is actually Blood minus the clotting factors like fibrinogen. Option C is incorrect because Lymph does not contain RBCs.
```
