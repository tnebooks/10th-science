Life originates from pre-existing life was showed by:

```markdown
  Louis Pasteur disproved the theory of spontaneous generation through his 
  famous swan-neck flask experiment. He demonstrated that microorganisms 
  only appear in nutrient broth after it has been exposed to the air 
  containing them, proving the principle of biogenesis: that life 
  originates only from pre-existing life.
```
