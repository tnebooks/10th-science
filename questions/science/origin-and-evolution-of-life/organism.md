The organism considered to be the fossil bird is __________

```markdown
  Archaeopteryx is widely considered the "fossil bird" because it represents 
  a transitional link between non-avian dinosaurs and modern birds. It 
  possesses avian features like feathers and wings, alongside reptilian 
  features such as a long bony tail, claws on its wings, and teeth in its jaws.
```
