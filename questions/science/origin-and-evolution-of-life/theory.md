explanation: >-
  The 'Theory of Use and Disuse' is a core part of Lamarckism. Jean-Baptiste
  Lamarck proposed that organs which are used extensively to cope with the
  environment become larger and stronger, while those that are not used
  deteriorate. He famously used the example of the giraffe's long neck to
  illustrate this concept.
