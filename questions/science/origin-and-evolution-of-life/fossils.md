- Uranium lead method

explanation: >-
  Radio-carbon dating ($C^{14}$ dating) is the most effective method for dating
  organic fossils of recent origin (up to about 50,000–60,000 years old). This
  is because the half-life of Carbon-14 is relatively short (5,730 years).
  Methods like Uranium-lead or Potassium-argon have much longer half-lives and
  are used for dating much older geological formations and ancient fossils.
