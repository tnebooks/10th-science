choices:
  - decreases
  - first decreases then increases
  - first increases and then decreases
answers:
  - increases
explanation: >-
  According to Le Chatelier's Principle, if a system at equilibrium is 
  disturbed, the system shifts to counteract that disturbance. By 
  periodically removing the product, the equilibrium shifts toward the 
  forward reaction to replace what was lost, thereby increasing the total 
  amount of product formed.
