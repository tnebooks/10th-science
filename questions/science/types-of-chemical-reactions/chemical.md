In a chemical equilibrium, the concentrations of reactants and products are ___________

```markdown
  At chemical equilibrium, the rate of the forward reaction equals the 
  rate of the reverse reaction. Because these rates are balanced, the 
  net concentrations of the reactants and products do not change over 
  time, though they are not necessarily equal to each other.
```
