answers:
  - the flowers are brightly coloured, have smell and nectar.
explanation: >-
  Anemophilous plants are pollinated by wind. Since wind does not need to 
  be attracted by visual or olfactory cues, these flowers are generally 
  small, dull-colored, and lack nectar or scent. Bright colors and nectar 
  are characteristics of entomophilous (insect-pollinated) flowers.
