In humans, a male and a female gamete fuse and form the zygote. The condition of zygote is -----

```markdown
  In humans, both the male gamete (sperm) and the female gamete (ovum) 
  are haploid ($n$), meaning they each contain 23 chromosomes. When they 
  fuse during fertilization, they form a zygote with two sets of 
  chromosomes ($2n$), resulting in a diploid condition with a total 
  of 46 chromosomes.
```
