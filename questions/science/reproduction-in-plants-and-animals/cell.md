---
choices:
  - Meiosis
  - Amitosis
answers:
  - Mitosis
explanation: >-
  In a mature pollen grain, the generative cell undergoes mitosis to 
  produce two male gametes. It is important to note that while meiosis 
  occurs earlier during microsporogenesis to form the microspores, the 
  subsequent division within the pollen grain itself is mitotic.
