According to the dental formula ____ kind of teeth is absent in rabbit.

```markdown
  Rabbits are herbivorous animals and do not have canine teeth. The dental 
  formula of a rabbit is $\frac{2033}{1023}$. The gap between the incisors 
  and the premolars where the canines would normally be is called the 
  diastema.
```
