The scattered light in Raman scattering contains _________ lines

```markdown
  Raman scattering involves the interaction of photons with matter. It 
  results in three types of lines: Rayleigh lines (no change in frequency), 
  Stokes lines (lower frequency/longer wavelength), and Anti-Stokes lines 
  (higher frequency/shorter wavelength).
```
