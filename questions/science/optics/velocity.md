choices:
  - VB = VG = VR
  - VB > VG > VR
  - VB < VG > VR
answers:
  - VB < VG < VR
---


If VB, VG, VR be the velocity of blue, green and red light respectively in a glass prism, 
then which of the following statement gives the correct relation?

```markdown
  In a glass prism, different colors of light travel at different speeds. 
  Red light has the longest wavelength and is deviated the least, meaning it 
  travels the fastest ($V_R$ is highest). Blue light has a shorter wavelength, 
  is deviated more, and travels the slowest ($V_B$ is lowest). Therefore, the 
  correct order of velocities is $V_B < V_G < V_R$.
```
