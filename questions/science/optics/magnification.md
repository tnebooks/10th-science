answer:
  - "either positive or negative"
explanation: "Magnification ($m$) of a convex lens can be positive or negative depending on the object's position. It is **negative** when the image is real and inverted (object placed beyond the focus). It is **positive** when the image is virtual and erect (object placed between the focus and the optical center)."
