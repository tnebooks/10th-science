Lightning is seen and thunder is heard after 9.8 seconds. If the speed of sound in air is 300 m s$^{-1}$, find the height of the clouds.

```markdown
Velocity relation:
$v = \frac{d}{t}$

Given:
$t = 9.8 \text{ s}$
$v = 300 \text{ m s}^{-1}$

Substituting:
$d = v \times t$

$d = 300 \times 9.8$

$d = 2940 \text{ m}$

Therefore:
Height of the clouds, $d = 2940 \text{ m}$
```
