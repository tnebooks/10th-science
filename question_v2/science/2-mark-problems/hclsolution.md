Calculate the pH of a 0.001 M hydrochloric acid (HCl) solution.

```markdown
HCl is a strong acid, so:
$[H^+] = 1.0 \times 10^{-3}$

pH formula:
$pH = -\log_{10}[H^+]$

Substituting:
$pH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-3})$

$\log_{10}(10^{-3}) = -3$

$pH = -(-3)$

$pH = 3$

Therefore:
pH value = 3
```
