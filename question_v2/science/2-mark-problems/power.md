If the power of a lens is -2 dioptre, find its focal length.

```markdown
The relation between power and focal length is:
$P = \frac{1}{f}$

Given:
$P = -2 \text{ D}$

Substituting:
$f = \frac{1}{P}$

$f = \frac{1}{-2}$

$f = -0.5 \text{ m}$

Therefore:
Focal length of the lens, $f = -0.5 \text{ m}$
```
