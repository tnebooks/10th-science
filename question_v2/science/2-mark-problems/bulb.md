A 100 W, 200 V electric bulb is connected in a circuit. Find the current flowing through it and its resistance.

```markdown
Power relation:
$P = VI$

Given:
$P = 100 \text{ W}$
$V = 200 \text{ V}$

Current:
$I = \frac{P}{V}$

$I = \frac{100}{200}$

$I = 0.5 \text{ A}$

Resistance:
$R = \frac{V}{I}$

$R = \frac{200}{0.5}$

$R = 400\ \Omega$

Therefore:
Current = $0.5 \text{ A}$
Resistance = $400\ \Omega$
```
