Calculate the number of moles in 46 g of sodium.

```markdown
Formula for number of moles:
$\text{Moles} = \frac{\text{Mass}}{\text{Atomic mass}}$

Given:
Mass = 46 g  
Atomic mass of sodium = 23  

Substituting:
$\text{Moles} = \frac{46}{23}$

$\text{Moles} = 2$

Therefore:
Number of moles = 2 moles
```
