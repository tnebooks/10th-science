Calculate the pH of a $1.0 \times 10^{-4}$ M $NaOH$ solution.

```markdown
pOH formula:
$pOH = -\log_{10}[OH^-]$

Given:
$[OH^-] = 1.0 \times 10^{-4}$

Substituting:
$pOH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-4})$

$pOH = 4$

Relation between pH and pOH:
$pH + pOH = 14$

$pH = 14 - pOH$

$pH = 14 - 4$

$pH = 10$

Therefore:
pH value = 10
```
