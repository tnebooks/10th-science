If the hydroxide ion concentration in a solution is $1.0 \times 10^{-11}$ mole, find its pH value.

```markdown
pOH formula:
$pOH = -\log_{10}[OH^-]$

Given:
$[OH^-] = 1.0 \times 10^{-11}$

Substituting:
$pOH = -\log_{10}(1.0 \times 10^{-11})$

$pOH = 11$

Relation between pH and pOH:
$pH + pOH = 14$

$pH = 14 - pOH$

$pH = 14 - 11$

$pH = 3$

Therefore:
pH value = 3
```
