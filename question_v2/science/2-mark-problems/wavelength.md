A sound wave of frequency 200 Hz travels with a velocity of 400 m s$^{-1}$ in a medium. Find its wavelength.

```markdown
Wave velocity relation:
$v = n \lambda$

Given:
$n = 200 \text{ Hz}$
$v = 400 \text{ m s}^{-1}$

Substituting:
$\lambda = \frac{v}{n}$

$\lambda = \frac{400}{200}$

$\lambda = 2 \text{ m}$

Therefore:
Wavelength, $\lambda = 2 \text{ m}$
```
