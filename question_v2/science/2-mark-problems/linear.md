A body of mass 5 kg has a linear momentum of 2.5 kg m s$^{-1}$. Calculate its velocity.

```markdown
Linear momentum is given by the formula:
$P = mv$

Given:
Mass, $m = 5 \text{ kg}$
Linear momentum, $P = 2.5 \text{ kg m s}^{-1}$

Formula:
$v = \frac{P}{m}$

Substituting:
$v = \frac{2.5}{5}$

Therefore:
Velocity, $v = 0.5 \text{ m s}^{-1}$
```
