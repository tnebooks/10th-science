A sound source of frequency 500 Hz approaches a listener with a speed of 30 m s$^{-1}$. If the speed of sound in air is 330 m s$^{-1}$, find the apparent frequency heard by the listener.

```markdown
Doppler effect formula:
$n' = \left(\frac{v}{v - v_s}\right) n$

Given:
$n = 500 \text{ Hz}$
$v = 330 \text{ m s}^{-1}$
$v_s = 30 \text{ m s}^{-1}$

Substituting:
$n' = \frac{330}{330 - 30} \times 500$

$n' = \frac{330}{300} \times 500$

$n' = \frac{33}{30} \times 500$

$n' = 1.1 \times 500$

$n' = 550 \text{ Hz}$

Therefore:
Apparent frequency, $n' = 550 \text{ Hz}$
```
