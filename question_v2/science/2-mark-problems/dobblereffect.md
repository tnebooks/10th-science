A sound source of frequency 90 Hz approaches a stationary observer with a speed equal to $\frac{1}{10}$ of the speed of sound. Find the apparent frequency heard by the observer.

```markdown
Doppler effect formula:
$n' = \left(\frac{v}{v - v_s}\right) n$

Given:
$n = 90 \text{ Hz}$
$v_s = \frac{v}{10}$

Substituting:
$n' = \frac{v}{v - \frac{v}{10}} \times n$

$n' = \frac{v}{\frac{9v}{10}} \times n$

$n' = \left(\frac{10}{9}\right) \times n$

$n' = \left(\frac{10}{9}\right) \times 90$

$n' = 100 \text{ Hz}$

Therefore:
Apparent frequency, $n' = 100 \text{ Hz}$
```
