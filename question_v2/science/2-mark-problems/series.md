Three resistors of $5\ \Omega$, $3\ \Omega$, and $2\ \Omega$ are connected in series to a 10 V battery. Find the total resistance and the current in the circuit.

```markdown
Total resistance in series:
$R_s = R_1 + R_2 + R_3$

Given:
$R_1 = 5\ \Omega$
$R_2 = 3\ \Omega$
$R_3 = 2\ \Omega$
$V = 10 \text{ V}$

Substituting:
$R_s = 5 + 3 + 2$

$R_s = 10\ \Omega$

Current:
$I = \frac{V}{R_s}$

$I = \frac{10}{10}$

$I = 1 \text{ A}$

Therefore:
Total resistance = $10\ \Omega$
Current = $1 \text{ A}$
```
